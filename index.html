Curriculum Vitae

My name is Rick Sanchez from Earth (Dimension C-137) the widowed father of Beth Smith, and the grandfather of Morty and Summer Smith. After the death of my daughter Beth and apparent death of my wife Diane in my home, I developed a portal gun and spent the following decades travelling the infinite multiverse in search of the specific alternate version of myself whom I believe is responsible for killing them, befriending Birdperson and becoming a leading figure in the revolution against the Galactic Federation throughout my mid-30s.

After being rejected romantically by Birdperson, I return to my journey of vengeance, embarking on a series of meaningless relationships before ultimately becoming the leader of a fledgling "Citadel of Ricks", originally formed to oppose me after I had killed a number of myself on his journey of vengeance, after abandoning said journey and binding the Ricks of the multiverse into a "Central Finite Curve" in which we are all the "Smartest Man in the Universe", manipulating the flow of realities in-which my daughter lived to ensure that she meet Jerry Smith and produce an endless number of hypothetical grandchildren, allowing them to hide from the Federation using their brainwaves.

In my depressed stage, I abandons the Citadel and casts myself into the multiverse once again and crashes into the garage of a now-adult version of Beth from a reality where she was not killed and her version of me had abandoned her twenty years ago. I befriending her and my hypothetical grandchildren and frequently traveling on adventures through space, visiting other planets and dimensions with them, but most commonly Morty Smith.

Powers and Abilities

  • I'm incredibly intelligent and frequently mentioned as the smartest human being in any one universe.
  • I have made countless discoveries and invented numerous gadgets and substances.
  • I'm specialized in advanced robotics and could easily work through or around most obstacles.
  • Long-term planning, deception and manipulation are effortless tasks for me, when I saw through a multilayered Zigerion simulation and pretended to fall for it and simultaneously destroyed the Council, collapsed the Federation and triggered my daughter's divorce in one fell swoop.
  • I'am also incredibly clever, adaptable, resourceful and capable of turning even a hopeless situation to my favor with nothing but my wits and some basic materials.
  • Despite my advanced age and unhealthy habits I still retain impressive strength and endurance.
  • I have extensive cybernetic and bionic enhancements.
  • I'm proficient at combat and could effectively use just about any melee or projectile weapon I can get my hands on or make.
    I'm also a magnificent and excellent trap builder.

He attempted to fix this, by using another potion, but this only made them mutate again, cronenberging the entire world. He couldn't fix the mutations in the end, and just left the dimension with Morty, leaving all of the mutated humans behind.

  • Cable Piracy: In the episodes Rixty Minutes and Interdimensional Cable 2: Tempting Fate, Rick used an interdimensional cable box to stream an infinite amount of TV channels to his own television and the intergalactic hospital television.
  • Time Manipulation: In the episode A Rickle in Time, Rick froze time for the equivalent of six months which caused time to start splitting and dividing multiple times, to the point where a Fourth Dimensional Being from the Galactic Federation came and tried to arrest him and his grandkids, also revealing that Rick may have done this before.
